DESCRIPTION

r.dsm.import.hb downloads digital surface models (DSM, in German DOM) for Bremen and Bremerhaven (HB) and area of interest. The data "Digitales Oberflächenmodell Bremen" is downloaded from METAVER and can be used under the specification of the licence: Landesamt GeoInformation Bremen, Creative Commons Namensnennung 4.0 International.

EXAMPLE

Bremen example

Download and import DSM with native resolution: 
r.dsm.import.hb aoi=aoi output=dsm -r
